Microsoft terminates donations lawmakers who opposed Biden's certification

John Emerald - February 04 2021

Microsoft corp said on friday that it will suspend all donations by its political actions commitee (pac) through 2022 to all U.S lawmakers who voted to object to the certification of Joe Biden's election as president
